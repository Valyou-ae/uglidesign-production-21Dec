### Critical Seams
- ✅ **Inner leg seam (inseam)** - Front to back alignment
- ✅ **Outer leg seam** - Side continuity
- ✅ **Center front seam** - Left/right leg meet
- ✅ **Center back seam** - Seat area alignment
- ⚠️ Waistband seam (recommended)

- **Recommended tile:** 3" × 3" (900 × 900 px)
- **Min:** 1.5" × 1.5" | **Max:** 6" × 6"

- ⚠️ **Account for 15-20% stretch distortion**
- Avoid horizontal stripes (emphasize curves)
- Vertical/diagonal patterns are flattering
- Dark patterns are more forgiving
- Avoid text or logos that will distort

## SEAM ALIGNMENT PRIORITY

### Always Critical (Must Align)
- Center front seams
- Side seams (body)
- Inner leg seams (leggings)
- Pocket alignments

### Recommended (Should Align)
- Shoulder seams
- Hood attachment
- Waistband connection
- Leg openings

### Not Required
- Handle attachments
- Edge hems
- Zipper areas

## CONSTRUCTION TYPE REFERENCE

### Cut & Sew
- Fabric printed FIRST as flat sheets
- Then cut to pattern pieces
- Then sewn together
- **Pros:** Full coverage, no white spots
- **Products:** All apparel, Tote bag

### Sublimation on Blank
- Design transferred to pre-made item
- Heat press sublimation process
- **Pros:** Lower cost, faster production
- **Products:** Pillow, Blanket, Beach Towel
