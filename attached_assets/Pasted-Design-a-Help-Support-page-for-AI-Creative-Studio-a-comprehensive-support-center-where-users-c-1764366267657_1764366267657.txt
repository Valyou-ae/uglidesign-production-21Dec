2. Q: "What's the difference between quality levels?"
   A: "Draft is fastest and good for quick previews. Standard offers balanced quality and speed. Premium provides maximum quality with more detail. Ultra is our highest quality with extreme detail, best for final outputs."

3. Q: "How do the 5 AI agents work together?"
   A: "Each agent has a specific role: Text Sentinel checks spelling and text, Style Architect enhances your prompt with style keywords, Visual Synthesizer generates the image, Master Refiner improves quality, and Quality Analyst scores and learns from your feedback."

4. Q: "Can I use generated images commercially?"
   A: "Yes, all images generated on paid plans can be used commercially. Free plan users have limited commercial rights. See our Terms of Service for full details."

5. Q: "Why did my generation fail?"
   A: "Generations can fail due to content policy violations, server issues, or connectivity problems. Check our Troubleshooting guide for specific error codes and solutions."

6. Q: "How do I cancel my subscription?"
   A: "You can cancel your subscription anytime from Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription. Your plan will remain active until the end of your current billing period."

7. Q: "What file formats can I download?"
   A: "You can download generated images in PNG, JPEG, or WebP formats. Mockups can be downloaded as PNG or JPEG. Background-removed images are available as PNG with transparency."

8. Q: "How do I get a refund?"
   A: "We offer refunds within 7 days of purchase for unused credits. Contact our support team with your order details to request a refund."
